Project Evaluations- SFCC

The Evaluations will be in rooms 219 and 220 at SFCC.

If you are coming from the north of Santa Fe, take US 285/84 into Santa Fe, which turns into St. Francis Drive. Continue south on St. Francis Drive to Rodeo Road. Turn right (West) onto Rodeo Road until you reach Richards Avenue. Turn left (South) onto Richards Avenue. Follow this road under I-25 until you reach Santa Fe Community College at 6401 Richards Avenue.

If you are coming from the north on I-25 into Santa Fe, exit North on St. Francis exit. Continue until you reach Rodeo Road, and turn left onto Rodeo Road. Continue on Rodeo Road until you reach Richards Avenue. Turn south onto Richards Avenue. Follow this road under I-25 until you reach Santa Fe Community College at 6401 Richards Avenue.

If you are coming from the south on I-25 into Santa Fe, exit on 599 south and turn left onto Highway 14. Turn right on Rancho Viejo Boulevard, left on to Ave Del Sur, and left on to Richards Avenue until you reach Santa Fe Community College at 6401 Richards Avenue.

Once you get to the college, go in toward the courtyard into the West Wing or directly into the West Wing.

The rooms are on the west side of the upper level of the West Wing.
