Expo and Awards Ceremony Schedule

Here is the (tentative) schedule of events for April 2020:


Wednesday, April 1
  Final reports due
  Students/teachers register to come to the Expo

Monday, April 13
  Nominations for the Best teacher sponsor sent to Consult

Wednesday, April 15
  Judges conference call - about half a dozen finalists will be determined.

Monday, April 20
  Submissions for the Best Web presentation of a final report due

Sunday, April 26
  Teams travelling more than 120 miles  check in at the hotel
  Relax on your own in the evening



Monday, April 27
  7:00  Breakfast at the hotel
  8:00  The previously announced finalists will give 25 minute
          presentations to Finalist judges between 8:00 and 12:30
          at TBA 
        Everyone else will set up their posters and give their
          presentations to Expo judges at pre-assigned half-an-hour
          time slots between 8:30 and 11:30 in the TBA 
        After their presentations, teams will be able to take tours
  9:00-12:00 Scholarship interviews with Scholarship judges
  11:30 Expo judges caucus
  11:30 Lunch
  12:30 Expo judges announce additional finalists
  1:00-4:00 Expo finalists make 25 minute presentations to Finalist judges
  1:00-5:00 Tours 
            Students and teachers vote for best project, technical poster,
            and logo during lunch or the tours.
  5:00-6:40  Dinner (on your own)
  7:00-8:00  
  

Tuesday, April 28
 7:30  Breakfast at the hotel
 8:30  Travel to Awards Ceremony
 9:00-noon  Awards Ceremony in the TBA 
12:00  Lunch Reception and media interviews
 1:00  Depart
